Awareness

  • We are inspired to make a difference to our world because as it stands now most people aren't aware of the current situation of the world's waste.We hope to convey the sheer amount of waste in the world to make people more aware of the amount of trash that currently exsits. Mr Beast was one of the main inspirations and motivation for us as we hope to help communities around the world with our site.

  • The goal of Worlds Waste Management is to create an easy to use center of information that organizations around the world can use to provide help to communities that need help with waste management. Using the provided information, organizations can provide short term improvements such as helping develop a waste management system. In conclusion, World Waste Management hopes to provide information that will lead to communities developing and maintaining a proper waste management system.
